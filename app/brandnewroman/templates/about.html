Brand New Roman

Brand New Roman is an open type SVG color font. This a vector font format that allows for multicolored fonts where the color is embedded directly in the individual letters.

Currently color fonts are supported in Adobe Photoshop/illustrator/InDesign CC 2018+, and display in Firefox and Edge; Safari support is coming in September. We have also made a black&white version of the font for use on unsupported platforms.

Brand New Roman was made by Hello Velocity. We are a New York based web studio, specializing in experimental marketing.

Brand New Roman is free to download, in both color and black & white versions. We'd love you to use it. Send us screenshots or share on Instagram under #BrandNewRoman so we can see what you make!
